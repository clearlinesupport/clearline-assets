About Clearline

I'm Andre, the tech behind the work and this site. Clearline exists to get things fixed, installed, or cleaned up without the red tape.

Clearline Field Systems LLC is a one-person outfit based in Rensselaer County. I handle low-voltage cleanup, ADA fixes, gear installs, and walkthroughs for the kinds of places that can't afford downtime — public offices, nonprofits, and small shops.

Everything’s flat-rate, insured, and documented. If something’s out of spec or left behind, I’ll sort it. If it needs to be installed right, I’ll do that too — and leave you with photos or a log if it helps.

  • ✔ Mission Aligned – I support work that matters: city offices, clinics, nonprofits, housing agencies
  • ✔ Experienced – Every service on this site is something I’ve done, not something I outsourced
  • ✔ Process First – Clean work, clear notes, no hidden gaps. You know what got done and what’s next
  • ✔ Paperwork in Hand – W9, COI, UEI, quotes — all set up so you can file and move on

This isn’t managed services. It’s just real-world tech support — wired in, shown up, and handled right the first time. That's what I do.
